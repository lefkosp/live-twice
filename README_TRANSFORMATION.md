# Live Twice Website Transformation - Complete

## Executive Summary

The Live Twice website has been completely transformed to maintain a **secretive, discreet, word-of-mouth** positioning. The new site reveals nothing about your competitive advantages while creating intrigue and demonstrating quality through restraint.

---

## What Changed

### Before
- 11 detailed sections explaining everything
- Case studies with metrics and tactics
- Detailed methodology explanations
- Fan account strategies revealed
- Paid advertising approaches shown
- Multiple CTAs competing for attention

### After
- 4 minimal sections (Home, About, Artists, Contact)
- Zero methodology details shared
- No tactical information revealed
- Single focused CTA on every page
- Pure positioning and social proof
- Creates curiosity without explanation

```

```
